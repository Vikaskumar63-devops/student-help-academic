FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the work 100% plagiarism-free?▼
Yes, absolutely. Every document is checked using plagiarism detection tools before delivery. You will receive a plagiarism report along with your work as proof.
How long does it take to complete a project?▼
Delivery time depends on the complexity of the project. Simple assignments can be done in 24–48 hours, while research papers and software projects may take 3–7 days. We always confirm the timeline before you pay.
How do I pay for the service?▼
We accept UPI, bank transfer, and other digital payment methods. Payment is typically done after you approve the quote and before we begin the work.
Is my information kept confidential?▼
Absolutely. We treat all client details — your name, project topic, and contact information — with complete confidentiality. We never share your data with third parties.
Can I request revisions after delivery?▼
Yes! We offer unlimited revisions until you are completely satisfied with the output. Just share your feedback and we'll make the necessary changes promptly.
Do you provide an explanation video for projects?▼
Yes, a project explanation video is provided with every software-based project. This helps you understand and present the project confidently during viva or demos.
Which subjects and domains do you cover?▼
We cover a wide range of subjects including Computer Science, IT, Electronics, MBA, MCA, and more. Software projects span Web Development, Machine Learning, IoT, Data Science, and other domains.
